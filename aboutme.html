About Me in General

Born in Poland, I have lived in the US for the last fourteen years (from the Midwest, through the Northeast, to the Southeast). Originally a researcher in the field of neuroscience I have worked as a visual designer for several years, and have most recently been focusing on UX and UI design.

Meaningful contribution to the community both locally and globally is a major motivator for my endeavors. In order to make those contributions successfully, I strive for excellence as a professional and as a human being.

Where I Live and What I do

I currently live in North Carolina, but I always think fondly of my Polish hometown, Cracow.

I am thrilled to be part of an exciting research team at NC State University in Raleigh where I serve as a visual/UX/Ui designer. We design and develop diagnostic/assessment tools for middle school math learning.
